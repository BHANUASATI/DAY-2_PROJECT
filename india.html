My Dream Place To Visit In India

Below is a List of some my favourite place in India.

    • Jammu Kashmir

      Known for its breathtaking landscape and lush gardans.

    • Darjeeling

      Famous for its tea plantations, stunning views of the Kanchenjunga, and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

    • Udaipur

      Known as the 'City of Lakes,' famous for its palaces, and romantic boat rides on Lake Pichola.

    • Goa

      Renowned for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.
